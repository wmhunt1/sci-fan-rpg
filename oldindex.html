Welcome to the world of placeholder.

Many years ago your quiet little village was destroyed by the evil forces of the Dark Lord, Evil Ploticus.

However, if you want to stand a chance you must first train and improve your skills.

To do so you will need to complete quests and earn gold.

Now go and explore the world.

But first, who are you?
